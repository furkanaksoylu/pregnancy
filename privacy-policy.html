Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Welcome to Mom Plus Pregnancy Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our pregnancy tracking and pill reminder mobile application (the "App").

Key Point: Your privacy is our priority. All your personal health data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Health and Pregnancy Information

The App collects the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Pregnancy Details: Pregnancy start date, estimated due date, and pregnancy progress
  • Medication Information: Pill names, dosages, frequencies, scheduled times, and colors
  • Daily Health Tracking: Water intake, prenatal vitamin compliance, and pill adherence records
  • Schedule Preferences: Wake-up and sleep times, notification preferences

1.2 Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage statistics (locally stored)
  • Notification delivery status

1.3 Purchase Information

If you subscribe to premium features, Apple handles all payment processing. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status from Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment details, credit card information, or Apple ID.

2. How We Use Your Information

All information is used exclusively to:

  • Provide pill tracking and reminder functionality
  • Display your pregnancy progress and baby development information
  • Send local notifications for medication reminders
  • Track your daily health habits (water intake, vitamins)
  • Personalize health program recommendations based on your pregnancy week
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your personal health data is stored locally on your device. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We cannot access your personal health information
  • Your data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)

3.2 No Cloud Sync

The App does not sync your data to any cloud servers. If you delete the App or switch devices, your data will not be transferred automatically.

3.3 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of locally stored data (where supported by the operating system)
  • Secure coding practices
  • Regular security updates

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Apple App Store (StoreKit)

For in-app purchases and subscriptions, we use Apple's StoreKit framework. Apple's privacy practices are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, available at https://www.apple.com/privacy/

4.2 No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your usage data remains private and on your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device, we have no access to share.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your data directly within the App
  • Modification: Edit or update your information at any time
  • Deletion: Delete individual records or all data by uninstalling the App
  • Notifications: Control notification preferences in the App and device settings
  • Export: Premium users can export their health data as PDF/CSV (if enabled in the App)

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

The App is available internationally. Since all data is stored locally on your device, no cross-border data transfer occurs. The App may support multiple languages.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy within the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Medical Disclaimer

Important: Mom Plus Pregnancy Tracker is a health tracking tool and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The information provided in the App, including nutrition plans, exercise recommendations, and baby development information, is for educational purposes only.

Always consult with your healthcare provider before making any decisions regarding your pregnancy, medications, or health regimen.
